Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find wall hangings in North Vancouver?
Wall hangings can be found in many home decor stores, art galleries, and online shops throughout North Vancouver.
How much do wall hangings typically cost in North Vancouver?
The price of wall hangings varies widely depending on size, material, craftsmanship, and other factors. In general, prices range from around $20 to several hundred dollars.
What types of wall hangings are available in North Vancouver?
A variety of wall hangings are available in North Vancouver including paintings, tapestries, sculptures, photographs, mirrors, and more.
Are there any unique or handmade wall hangings in North Vancouver?
Yes! There are a number of local artists and artisans who create one-of-a-kind pieces for sale throughout the city. These pieces may be found at markets or directly from the artists website/shop.
Are there any special considerations when buying a wall hanging for my home?
Yes - its important to consider not only aesthetics but also function when choosing a wall hanging for your home (i.e., will it fit the space?). Additionally you should make sure to buy something that is well made and safe for your walls (if hung with nails).
